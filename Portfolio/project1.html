About Project

Project done by: Adobe XD
Method: Mobile App.
ScootCart is an innovative native mobile app designed to enhance the in-flight purchasing experience for passengers and boost sales for Scoot Airlines.
The app addresses the challenges faced by both passengers and cabin crew members, streamlining the order-taking process and enabling real-time interaction. It features a digital F&B menu that allows passengers to explore offerings in their preferred language and provides staff with the ability to update menu items instantly. To ensure efficient communication without heavy reliance on Wi-Fi, ScootCart utilizes Bluetooth technology, allowing seamless interactions between passengers and staff. By promoting spontaneous purchases and enabling quick order fulfillment, ScootCart aims to drive higher in-flight sales, improve customer satisfaction, and enhance overall operational efficiency for Scoot Airlines.

Color Scheme

Scoot's UI features energetic colors (orange, yellow, and blue) reflecting their adventurous brand. Design principles of balance, unity, proximity, and emphasis create a cohesive, engaging, and intuitive interface, enhancing the in-flight shopping experience.

Typography

Scoot uses bold, playful fonts like Boogaloo for headings and Roboto for body text, reflecting their energetic brand. Clear visual hierarchy guides passengers through seamless ordering, ensuring easy comprehension and enhancing the purchasing process.

Hamburger Navigation

Hamburger navigation saves screen space, offering a clean and intuitive interface. Users can access menu options easily with a single tap, ensuring a seamless and clutter-free mobile app experience.
