About Project

Project done by: Adobe XD
Method: Mobile App.
My app aims to empower older individuals by equipping them with the necessary knowledge and skills to utilize online confidently and effectively and card payments when making purchases. Users will acquire knowledge about online and card payments, including terminology and security measures, boosting their self-assurance. Users will autonomously perform real-life payments, confidently interacting with interfaces and successfully completing transactions using online and card methods.

Color Scheme

The official colour of ProPay, a company, is a strong and sophisticated shade of blue that symbolizes confidence, dependability, and the contemporary nature of digital transactions. To create an appealing visual harmony, the company app utilizes a complementary colour scheme by combining corporate blue with a lively and enthusiastic accent colour. The chosen accent colour is a vibrant orange that injects a sense of energy and directs focus toward significant elements within the app.

Typography

Incorporating Gotham Bold font(For header or title/important word) in my app, I can create a visually impactful and professional look. Its bold and commanding presence helps establish a clear visual hierarchy, while its versatility and recognition add credibility. Maintain brand consistency and balance with complementary fonts for a cohesive and engaging design which is very helpful and stands out for my app.
