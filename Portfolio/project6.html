Portfolio

Skill Used: Low fidelity, logo design, prototype,animation, high fidelity. Anyone can see my portfolio app to understand more aboit me, they get to know more about me, portfolio and ways to contact me.

We decided to create 3 sections. About me will have 3 things, Portfolio will have 3 things and Contact will have 2.

I will be using Analogous Colour Scheme. The colour palette used will be blue and green. The mixture of blue and green indicates professionalism and reliability, building trust with potential customers or employers. Analogous hues create an aesthetically appealing background without overpowering my work, allowing my portfolio's content to stand out. Blue and green are user-friendly colour options for my portfolio website since they are both aesthetically pleasing and provide accessibility and readability. Blue and green hues are so versatile, I may change the colour scheme to fit the various moods and styles of my portfolio items. Incorporating Abril Fatface font (Used this for words more important than normal wording) improves my portfolio by adding impact, distinctiveness, and visual hierarchy. With bold headings and readable body text. Utilizing the Poppins font(for normal wordings) in my portfolio website offers versatility, a contemporary and clean design, a friendly and approachable aesthetic, excellent readability, and consistent typography. These elements enhance the user experience, create a welcoming vibe, and ensure a polished and modern appearance for my portfolio website. Incorporating Roboto font(For header or title/important word) in my portfolio website.
